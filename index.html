SERVICES

SUSTAINABLE SOLUTION FOR INDUSTRIES

DACC+U, or Direct Air Carbon Capture & Utilization, integrates Carbon Capture and Utilization with Direct Air Capture. This innovative approach efficiently captures CO2 from the atmosphere. Using a blower, air is drawn in and passed through an Absorption Tank. The CO2-saturated solvent is then processed through the Collector and Settler tank, leading to the Decomposition Reactor where a specialized catalyst decomposes the carbon. This process ensures effective decomposition and prepares the solvent for subsequent cycles in the Carbon Filtration Unit and regeneration tank. This method not only captures CO2 but also contributes to a sustainable future and producing byproducts with low energy consumption.
